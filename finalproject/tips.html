Hydration Tips

Top 5 Hydration Tips

  1. Drink water first thing in the morning
  2. Carry a reusable water bottle
  3. Set reminders to drink water throughout the day
  4. Eat water-rich foods like fruits and vegetables
  5. Flavor your water with natural ingredients like lemon or cucumber

Staying Hydrated During Exercise

During exercise, it's crucial to maintain proper hydration. Drink water before, during, and after your workout. For intense exercises lasting more than an hour, consider sports drinks to replenish electrolytes. Listen to your body and drink when you feel thirsty.
